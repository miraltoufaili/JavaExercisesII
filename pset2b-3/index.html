Women in Engineering

Dream big! There are no limits or restriction

This website is to help discover, mold, support and show every girl who has interest in engineering that SHE CAN DO IT!

In the United State, there is only 14% of engineers who are women (according to the Congressional Joint Economic Committee)

Resons behind this low number are:

  • Lack of female engineering role models
  • Misconceptions of what it is like to be an engineer
  • Not believing in their mental capabilities
